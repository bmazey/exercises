Welcome to our exercises repo!

Here is the content you need for our first week of class. We will work on
materials from the exercises folder during the afternoon sessions. The content
in the interview folder is an optional challenge assignment for you to do on
your own or with friends.

To complete these assignments, remember to look at the test classes first.
The test classes will tell you what we want your programs to do. TIP: look at
the method names! The method names will tell you what the test should DO. Then
look at the content inside assertEquals(...). assertEquals will tell you what
the result of the test should be.

For example: assertEquals(myPalindrome.isPalindrome("racecar"), true)
This means that the isPalindrome() method should return true for a "racecar",
which is a palindrome!

Once you know what your program is supposed to do and say, you can get to work
on making it all happen. Good luck!
